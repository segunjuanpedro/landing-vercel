About Me

I am a motion designer and 3D artist specialized in motion graphics, visual design, and creative development. I work on audiovisual projects and visual systems where narrative, animation, and technology converge.

Since 2020, I have been teaching Motion Graphics and 3D Animation at Da Vinci School, supporting both technical and creative learning processes and developing content focused on professional practice.

I have certified training in Motion Graphics (After Effects, Cinema 4D), 3D Modeling (ZBrush), Web Design (HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, WordPress), and programming fundamentals applied to creative development. I also have experience in Resin 3D Printing and Illustration projects.

I work freelance on visual projects and develop personal ventures related to design and object production, exploring the relationship between the digital and the physical.
